Thailand Trip : 3-6 January 2020

nsulistiyawan
In early 2020, me and my friends did a travel to Thailand. 

I only travelled to Singapore previously, and most of Singaporean can speak English, so.. while it's cool and fun, its not as challenging as Thailand. None from us can speak Thai language, yeah so we only use body language :)

We could find a lot of interesting spot on Thailand.

When there's so many of tourist attraction, there's one i couldn't find that in my country (Indonesia), it's Tiffany Show. I live and grow in Indonesia, a country with the largest muslim in the world, so a LGBT thing is considered a taboo / a wrong thing here.

Tiffany Show is a drama musical (& acrobatic) show where majority of the player are transgender. I couldn't lie, some of them are really beautiful, yup not all of them. Some of them also still looks like a boy/man for me. But still it was an awesome experience.

I learn that something that looks wrong in a country, might be a common in another country.
